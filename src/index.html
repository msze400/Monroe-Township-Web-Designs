About Us

Monroe Township Web Designs

Hi my name is Matt Sze. I'm the owner and sole developer of Monroe Township Web Designs and a Monroe Township native! I got into programming during my undergrad years at Rutgers where I became a MATLAB teaching assistant. Funny enough, I majored in chemical engineering and even worked professionally as a chemical engineer in the Oil and Gas industry for three years.

I quickly realized I did not want to do that for the rest of my life and rekindled my love of programming during the Covid pandemic where I attended Fullstack Academy's Immersive Software Engineering bootcamp. After completing the course I went off to work as a developer at Colgate Palmolive working on their eCommerce site. I eventually found my way to working at MIT Lincoln Labs in their Artificial Intelligence and Algorithms division in Boston.

I want to help clients expand their business and help them get the most out of their online presence.

Matt Sze CEO-Founder
