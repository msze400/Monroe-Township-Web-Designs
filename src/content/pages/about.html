About Us

Monroe Township Web Designs

Hi my name is Matt Sze. I'm the owner and sole developer of Monroe Township Web Designs. I got into programming during my undergrad years at Rutgers where I became a MATLAB teaching assistant. Funny enough, I majored in chemical engineering and even worked professionally as a chemical engineer in the Oil and Gas industry for three years.

I quickly realized I did not want to do that for the rest of my life and rekindled my love of programming during the Covid pandemic where I attended Fullstack Academy's Immersive Software Engineering bootcamp. After completing the course I went off to work as a developer at Colgate Palmolive working on their eCommerce sity. I eventually found my way to working at MIT Lincoln Labs in their Artificial Intelligence and Algorithms division in Boston.

My personal goal is to help small businesses who don't have thousands of dollars upfront to get a great website up and running with the latest and greatest design trends.

I want to help clients expand their business and help them get the most out of their online presence.

Matt Sze CEO-Founder
